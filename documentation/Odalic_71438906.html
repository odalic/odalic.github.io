ADEQUATE : Odalic

Created by Tomas Knap, last modified by István Satmári on Mai 15, 2017

The goal of the project is to (semi)automatically leverage tabular data to Linked Data. It uses and further improves TableMiner+, originally developed by Ziqi Zhang from University of Sheffield.

Proposal (draft): https://docs.google.com/document/d/15hqXzy7fjVrjOs8ASK8my9mVJe3LUXffAf1Ar32ywYg/edit#
